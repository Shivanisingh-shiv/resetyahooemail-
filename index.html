Security Question Requirements

When resetting your Yahoo security questions:

  • You must have access to your Yahoo account
  • Current password is required for verification
  • Choose questions only you can answer
  • Answers should be memorable but not easily guessable
Note: Security questions are used for account recovery when you can't access your email or phone.

How to Reset Security Questions

1

Sign In to Account Security

Go to Yahoo Account Security page and sign in with your email and password. Navigate to "Security Questions" section.

2

Verify Your Identity

Confirm your identity by entering your password again or completing two-step verification if enabled.

3

Update Your Questions

Select new security questions and provide answers. Choose questions that are meaningful only to you.

Security Best Practices

Unique Questions

Use questions that aren't easily found in public records or social media.

Memorable Answers

Choose answers you'll remember but others can't guess.

Alternative Recovery

Set up backup recovery options like secondary email or phone.

Regular Updates

Review and update your security questions periodically.

Troubleshooting Security Updates

Common Issues and Solutions

  • Forgot Password: Reset your password first before updating security questions
  • Verification Failed: Ensure you're entering correct account credentials
  • Changes Not Saving: Clear browser cache or try a different browser
  • Account Locked: Wait 24 hours if you've had multiple failed attempts
  • No Access to Account: Use account recovery options
Security Tip: Never share your security question answers with anyone. Yahoo will never ask for them outside of official account recovery.
